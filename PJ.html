Secretary General

Pariyal Jandyal

With profound enthusiasm, the secretariat extends a cordial welcome to the third iteration of the GD MUN. It is their distinct pleasure to introduce the esteemed Secretary General, Pariyal Jandyal, an astute leader possessing extensive experience in the realms of diplomacy, international politics, and world history. Her five-year odyssey has been marked by active involvement in Model United Nations, Youth Parliaments, Public Forums, and debates, establishing her as a proficient MUN participant and a consummate debater. Beyond her acumen in diplomacy and debate, Pariyal is an ardent philanthropist, imbuing her endeavors with a fervent commitment to enacting positive change. Her unwavering dedication has metamorphosed this conference from a mere concept into a tangible reality. Pariyal's leadership is characterized by her adeptness at discerning intricate nuances and blending creativity with innovation. This approach nurtures an environment conducive to the flourishing of novel ideas and diverse perspectives. Under Pariyal's astute guidance, GD MUN transcends its role as a mere assembly and assumes the guise of a confluence of intellects, mirroring her profound belief in the potency of diplomacy to foster constructive global transformations. As they embark on this intellectual voyage, the speaker holds a steadfast conviction that they will challenge established paradigms and make substantial contributions to a more enlightened and interconnected global community.
